Honey Buffalo Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 2lbs for boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Honey
  • Moor's Buffalo Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Cut chicken thighs into bite size pieces
  2. Season chicken thighs with all seasonings
  3. Cook in high-walled pan till chicken internal temp is 165 F
  4. Add in buffalo sauce and honey to taste
  5. Cut heat to low and allow sauce to reduce
